Limveld Awaits

A new Roguelike based in the Elden Ring universe Directed by Junya Ishizaki, his first time directing a team such as
FromSoft, Becoming the first eever fully multiplayer SoulsLike to date. With Ishizaki becoming the first to do it.

Play as more than 8 NightFarers, all giving the player more variety with weapons than the next,
Make your way through 3 dauntless nights, fighting various enemies along your way.
Making it to the third night and surviving, you will be given the chance to face, The NightLord.

Face The Night

Overcome your enemies and collect new weapons, as the threat is always looming throughout the night.
Night Falls across Limveld, a land that changes each time inbetween game sessions so stay on your feet.
Rise as the NightFarers and survive, making your way through the 3 days to face the boss at its end.
Each Nightfarer has three unique capacities, so use them to your benefit and learn your Nightfarer.

Survive The Everlasting Night.
Make your way through the Night, Making it to the end of the three days, Once all NightFarers are ready. Open the gate and bare withness to the
NightLord.
Each Nightlord must be faced and defeated before the reign of night can be ended, unlocking your next foe in your way.
Once a Nightlord is defeated you will be rewarded heavely. Then be given the option to move onto the next Nightlord.
